What format should videos be in?
most video format are supported, if you want to avoid server side transcoding, the recommended resolution is: 512 x 288 for 16:9 material and 448 x 336 for 4:3 material. Videos have to be encoded in Ogg Theora / Vorbis. more info on video encoding
